About Heart Disease Predictor

This is a project that uses data mining techniques on health care data to predict the outcomes. Cardiovascular diseases (CVDs) cause the majority of deaths in India. Heart disease and stroke are the major causes and are accountable for >80% of CVD deaths .
Here this project concentrates on classification models. This project uses some of the classification algorithms like decision tree algorithm and KNN algorithm to predict heart diseases and compare their performance.
Besides, this low cost model will cut down on a lot of expenses that would have otherwise been spent on various testing. But with this, the user just needs to fill in the form to get the result without having to spend on any tests.
